Here are questions and answers people frequently asked to us.

What services does your company provide?

Our company provides a wide range of security services, including security guard services, mobile patrols, and alarm response. We can tailor our services to meet the specific needs of your business or event.

How do you select and train your security guards?

All of our security guards are carefully selected and undergo thorough background checks and training. We ensure that our guards are knowledgeable, professional, and dedicated to providing the highest level of security.

Can you provide references from satisfied clients?

Yes, we would be happy to provide references from satisfied clients upon request. We take pride in the quality of our services and have many clients who are happy to speak to the effectiveness of our security guards.

What is your policy on response times?

We are committed to providing timely and efficient response to any security concerns. Our guards are trained to respond quickly and appropriately to any situation, and we have systems in place to ensure that our guards can be on-site as soon as possible in the event of an emergency.

Can you provide security for events and temporary locations?

Yes, we are experienced in providing security for a wide range of events and temporary locations. We can work with you to develop a customized security plan for your event or location, and provide the necessary guards and other security measures to ensure the safety of your guests and property.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer competitive rates for our security services and are happy to provide a quote based on your specific security needs. We can provide hourly, daily, or long-term rates, and are flexible in our pricing to meet your budget and security requirements.
